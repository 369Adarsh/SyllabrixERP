02 / PRINCIPLES

How Syllabrix should feel

Principles guide every visual and copy decision. When in doubt, choose the option that reinforces the one you are violating least.

P · 01

Confident, never corporate

We talk like the smartest person at a kirana counter, not like enterprise software. Direct verbs, real numbers, no "leverage" or "synergy."

P · 02

Earned trust

Money, taxes, and AI all need receipts. Show the source, the math, and the rollback. Never hide a destructive action behind a clever animation.

P · 03

Modular by default

The brick is the unit. Components stack, snap, and reorder without breaking. If a layout only works at one screen size, it's not finished.

P · 04

Warmth over polish

Cream over cold gray. Cyan as a spark, not a flood. The shop owner is on a 2-year-old Android with a cracked screen — design for that, not the design awards.
