Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Do I need to install anything?
No. Syllabrix is 100% cloud-based and runs in your browser on any device — phone, tablet, laptop, or desktop. No software installation, no Windows-only restrictions, no USB dongles.
How does WhatsApp billing work?
Syllabrix uses WhatsApp Web/App links to open pre-filled invoice messages for your customers. One click and the message is ready to send — no WhatsApp Business API fees, no template approvals, no monthly costs.
Is my data safe? Can I export it?
Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit, stored in isolated tenant databases with row-level security. You can export invoices as PDF, GSTR-1 as JSON/Excel, and customer lists as CSV at any time. Your data is always yours.
Can I use Syllabrix for my coaching centre or school?
Absolutely. Syllabrix has a dedicated Education ERP mode — fee management, student profiles, homework tracker, exam preparation planner, attendance, batch management, and parent WhatsApp reports. It's the only ERP that truly covers coaching centres.
Can I run multiple branches from one account?
Yes! The Pro plan supports multiple branches with independent stock, staff, and revenue tracking — but all visible from a single central dashboard. Each branch has its own RBAC with role-based access control.
What happens after the free trial?
After 14 days you can choose a paid plan to continue. If you don't upgrade, your account moves to read-only mode — you never lose your data. We'll remind you before the trial ends. No surprise charges, ever.
